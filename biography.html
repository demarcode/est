Meet Stacey and Lindsey



Lindsey Griffin



Lindsey Griffin is a proud lifelong resident of Chicago's Western Suburbs and it is an honor for her to serve the communities she loves so much. Helping others is what she does best. When it comes to professionalism and integrity, she delivers every time. Her partner Stacey Harvey delivers the same - creating a perfect pair providing first class service to each client.

Lindsey studied Business Administration and Psychology at the University of Kansas. Post college, she spent 10+ years in the exciting advertising agency world of Chicago. She represented several blue chip accounts where superior client service was always expected.

Lindsey and her husband Ryan have lived in Glen Ellyn with their three children since 2007. The Griffin Family is actively involved in many local school, sports, and charity organizations in the area. They adore Glen Ellyn and consider it a privilege to call this all-American town home.
